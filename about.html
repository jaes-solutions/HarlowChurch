Welcome to the Harlow Seventh-day Adventist Church

Established in 1954, our church has been an integral part of the vibrant community of Harlow. Rooted in the principles of the Seventh-day Adventist faith, we are committed to spreading the message of God's love and salvation to our members and the wider community.

At Harlow SDA Church, we strive to create a warm and welcoming environment where individuals and families can grow spiritually and find meaningful connections. Our diverse congregation reflects the inclusive nature of our beliefs, embracing people from all walks of life.

We offer a range of services and activities designed to enrich the spiritual journey of our members, including worship services, Sabbath School classes, prayer meetings, and community outreach initiatives. Through these endeavors, we seek to make a positive impact on our local community and beyond.

Whether you are new to the area or looking for a church home, we invite you to join us at Harlow SDA Church. Explore our website to learn more about our beliefs, upcoming events, and how you can get involved. We look forward to welcoming you into our fellowship!
