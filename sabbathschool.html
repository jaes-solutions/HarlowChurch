Children's Sabbath School

Our church is dedicated to nurturing young hearts and minds each Sabbath through age-appropriate Bible study and interactive classes. Children participate in engaging activities like songs, crafts, quizzes, games, and Bible stories. They are also welcome to join their parents during the main church service for a special children’s story segment.
