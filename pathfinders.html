Who Are Pathfinders?

When people hear "Pathfinders," they often think of marching, outdoor adventures, creative hobbies, and exploring nature. But beyond these, Pathfinders is a dynamic, character-building club created especially for children ages 10 to 15. Sponsored by the Seventh-day Adventist Church and open to youth of all backgrounds, Pathfinders inspires kids to discover more about the world and deepen their spiritual connection with God. With millions of members across the globe, Pathfinders is a thriving community of young adventurers.
