Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your personal information.

We only collect personal data that you voluntarily submit through our website forms, such as when contacting us or signing up for updates.

Your information is securely stored and only accessible to authorized personnel. We do not share your data with third parties.

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict how we use your personal information. Please contact us to exercise these rights.
