Prison Ministries

The Prison Ministries Department is passionately devoted to sharing messages of hope, redemption and transformation with those within correctional facilities. Through regular Bible study sessions, heartfelt musical performances, and inspirational talks, the team provides spiritual support and encouragement to individuals navigating incarceration. These efforts aim to nurture personal growth, help restore broken lives, and offer a renewed sense of purpose to those often forgotten by society.

Community Outreach

Our Community Outreach Ministry is at the heart of service in action—engaging with the needs of the local population through charitable events, practical assistance, and meaningful interactions. From distributing essential supplies and providing free health services to offering workshops that empower and uplift, the ministry exemplifies Christ’s love through genuine, hands-on compassion. It is a bridge between the church and the community, helping build stronger, more connected lives.

Eld. Donny Halder

Health Ministries

The Health Ministries Department promotes a wholistic approach to wellbeing, recognising that physical, mental and spiritual health are deeply intertwined. Through a variety of educational seminars, lifestyle programmes, and community health screenings, it empowers individuals to make informed choices towards healthier living. Rooted in Christian values, our health initiatives aim to support both preventive care and personal transformation.

Men's Ministries

Men’s Ministries provides a space for men to grow in faith, develop character, and lead with integrity within their families, workplaces, and the church. Through mentoring groups, prayer breakfasts, retreats and service projects, the ministry encourages men to live out their God-given roles with strength and humility. It seeks to cultivate spiritual resilience and foster meaningful relationships among men of all ages and backgrounds.
